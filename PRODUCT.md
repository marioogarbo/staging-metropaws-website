# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Filipino pet owners in Las Piñas, Parañaque, and Metro Manila. Middle to upper-middle class families who own dogs or cats and want structured, predictable pet healthcare without the anxiety of surprise vet bills. Tech-comfortable: they use phones for everything and expect QR codes and digital records to just work. They care deeply about their pets and respond to warmth, but they make decisions based on clarity and value.

## Product Purpose

MetroPaws is a pet wellness membership club offering a digital membership card, QR Pet ID, and service tracker. Free to register; paid annual plans (Standard, Deluxe, Premium) unlock expanded care coverage at partner clinics. The core promise: walk into any partner clinic with your pet's full profile, session history, and vaccination records already on your phone. No paperwork. No guessing what's covered. The Founding 50 program selects the first members personally, creating an early community with locked-in pricing and exclusive perks.

## Brand Personality

Premium. Exclusive. Confident.

MetroPaws does not beg for attention. It earns trust through clarity, restraint, and the quiet authority of a brand that knows exactly what it offers and who it's for.

## Anti-references

- **E-commerce pet shops**: product grids, sale badges, discount-first visual language, anything that looks like a clearance sale
- **Generic vet clinic sites**: clinical white and teal, stock photos of stethoscopes, no brand identity, impersonal and interchangeable

## Design Principles

1. **Quiet authority**: MetroPaws speaks with confidence, not urgency. Premium means restraint, not loudness. Decoration earns its place or gets cut.
2. **Show the product, not the category**: The QR ID, membership card, and session tracker are the story. Not "pet wellness" as an abstraction. Concrete over generic.
3. **Exclusivity through scarcity**: The Founding 50 signals rarity without gimmicks. Limited color. Limited ornamentation. Weight through what's left out.
4. **Trust through transparency**: Pricing, coverage, and clinic access must be unambiguous. No insurance-speak, no buried asterisks, no conditional language.
5. **Grounded in Filipino context**: Las Piñas, Metro Manila, barangay-level community. The design should feel rooted here, not imported wholesale from a Western SaaS template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Color contrast 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text and UI components. Touch targets minimum 44x44px for mobile. Reduced-motion preference respected for any animations.
